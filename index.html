Why Financial Calculators Are Important

Financial decisions such as taking a loan or starting an investment plan have long-term consequences. A small difference in interest rate, tenure or monthly contribution can result in lakhs of rupees difference over time.

Financial calculators help you visualize these differences before committing your money. They allow you to compare scenarios, test assumptions and choose options that align with your financial goals.

Common Financial Mistakes to Avoid

  • Choosing long loan tenures just to reduce EMI
  • Ignoring total interest paid over the loan period
  • Delaying investments instead of starting early
  • Not increasing investments as income grows
  • Relying on assumptions instead of calculations
